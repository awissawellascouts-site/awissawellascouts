Our History

A glorious timeline of the Awissawella District Scouts legacy, spanning generations of leadership and service.

1921

The Beginnings

The first seeds of scouting form within the local schools of Awissawella, bringing Baden Powell's vision to the area.

1954

Official Recognition

Awissawella District is formally inaugurated as an independent administrative scout district in Sri Lanka.

1982

The Golden Jamboree

Hosted the first major regional Jamboree, connecting thousands of local scouts.

2005

Community Service Awards

District scouts participated heavily in disaster relief, fundamentally changing our community impact scale.

2026

Digital Expansion (Awissawella 2.0)

Launch of the new e-library and integrated digital portal, giving every scout immediate access to rank advancement guides and logbooks.
